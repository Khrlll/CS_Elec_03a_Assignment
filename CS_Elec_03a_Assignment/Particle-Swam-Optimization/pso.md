Description:
PSO is inspired by the behavior of birds flocking or fish schooling.
Each solution (called a particle):

*Moves around in the search space
*Learns from its own experience (personal best)
*Learns from others (global best)

PSO is simpler than GA and works well for continuous optimization problems.

Examples:
1. Optimizing Neural Network Weights
PSO adjusts weights to minimize error in predictions.

2. Engineering Design (e.g., minimizing cost)
Example: Designing a bridge with minimum material but maximum strength.
